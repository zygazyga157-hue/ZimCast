Technical Flow
FROM POCKETS HILL
TO YOUR
PALM.

A four-step bridge that turns ZBC's broadcast into a mobile-ready stream, delivered to your phone wherever you are in Zimbabwe.

1
Source — ZBC Studios

ZBC broadcasts their live signal from Pockets Hill studio via satellite or fibre.

2
Encoder — Hardware Capture

A hardware encoder at the studio converts the feed into HLS/DASH — the format mobile phones understand.

3
Cloud — AWS Distribution

The digital feed is pushed to AWS cloud, which handles delivery, ad-insertion and high-concurrency PPV access at scale.

4
App — ZimCast

ZimCast calls the cloud link and delivers it to your screen — live, personalised, in the resolution your data plan can handle.
